## Sore High Landing Page (SHLP)
This landing page is for SoreHigh shirt. Since it is not a registered buisiness under Malaysia law, there is no SSM, buisiness finance account. This is page is purely for people to browse SoreHigh products, available models / cuttings / color, manually contact the seller (Aziz) via Whatsapp to place their order.

## SHLP flow
1. Header    
- Consist of SoreHigh logo, 3 buttons (Home, Product, Contact) and 'Stalk Us' button
- Each of the 3 button is clickable and directs user to the respective section (Home > Header + Hero section, Product > Product section, Contact > Contact section). 
- The 'Stalk Us' button direct user to SoreHigh official instagram link.

2. Hero 
- Simple 'Train Today Move Forever' animated text header.
- Simple about of what is SoreHigh, what products, what style it carries etc that describe Sore High lifestyle wears.

3. Products
- Simple 'Product' animated text header with 'selected works and product' side text
- Section consisting of clickable ProductItemCard. This component has a product title, product text and  smooth horizontal auto swiping of images of corresponding product images array.
- Upon user clicking on the ProductItemCard component, user will be directed to ProductPage to view a full display of the clicked item. In this page, there is a back button (clicking direct user back to landing page of where they previously click, aka the clicked product), a horizontal swipable product images, product title, price, "interested? click here to preorder now!" that links to Aziz whatsapp chat. Additionally, a sharable / copyable text is provided above the "interested? click here to preorder now!" to allow user to conviniently copy and paste the product item name and link to be used when chatting with Aziz via Whatsapp personally later or to share it with others. For example: "Checkout this product <itemName> by SoreHigh: <ProductPage link>".

4. Size Chart 
- Simple 'Size Chart' animated text header with 'Size curated to for all hustlers' dscription below.
- Section displays a size chart png.

5. Testimonials
- Simple 'What People Say' with side text 'Over time, some of them have become a certified sore high!'
- Slider or customer's testimony alongside their name, role, product purchased and instagram account with link.

6. FAQ 
- Simple 'Layaning your sore high question' 
- Section consist of a list of clickable expandable FAQCard
- FAQCard orriginally is just a rectagular card with the question statement and a '+' button. Clicking on the '+' button expands the card to reveal the answer to the question.

7. Worked With
- Simple header 'Worked with some of the best sore highs out there' 
- Horizontally scrolling carousel with link navigation to respective parties

8. Contact 
- Simple 'Enough Talk, Contact Us' header with 'Place your orders, dms collaboration or borak'.
- Section displays SoreHigh contacts.
